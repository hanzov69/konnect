1. Choose your printer type

Prusa Connect's SDK does not expose a "generic Klipper" type — every printer must advertise as one of Prusa's own. This choice affects which UI Connect shows you, but does not require real Prusa hardware. You can safely pick the profile that most closely matches your printer.

2. Printer identity

Serial number is how Connect recognizes this printer across reboots. A default is generated from your hardware; feel free to override. Once you register in step 4, this cannot be changed without unregistering first.

3. Register with Prusa Connect

Click Get code. A one-time code will appear. Visit connect.prusa3d.com/add (or scan the QR), sign in, and paste the code. This page will update automatically once registration finishes.
